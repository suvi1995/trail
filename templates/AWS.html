AWS Training Course Syllabus

Softllogic’s AWS course syllabus has been framed by AWS and cloud experts after assessing the recruitment needs and the issues faced by the industry. We constantly update the syllabus to be abreast with latest AWS upgrades. We ensure that you will be gaining knowledge on the latest cloud technology.

The syllabus is prepared so that the students are the ideal candidates that the organizations expect. The syllabus is also holistic with the fundamentals of Amazon S3, EC2 etc and also other services of AWS. The syllabus is updated once every batch completes so as to comprise latest developments in AWS and take away the outdated topics.
